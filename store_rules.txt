## How to use this file
##
## Add rules for the specific stores/retailers you buy from.
## The agent reads this file and includes it in Claude's instructions.
## Be as specific as possible about receipt formats so Claude parses them correctly.
##
## Delete these comments and replace with your own store rules. Examples below.
##
## ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
## EXAMPLE: WALMART RECEIPTS
## - Item format: product name on one line, price on the next
## - "ST#" at the top = store number, ignore it
## - OrderID = "TC#" (transaction code) at the bottom
## - Date at the top in MM/DD/YYYY format
## - SUBTOTAL = pre-tax, TAX = combined GST+PST, TOTAL = final
##
## EXAMPLE: COSTCO RECEIPTS
## - Items listed with item number, description, then price
## - "A" or "E" after price = taxable item
## - Member number at top — ignore it
## - OrderID = last 4 digits of register + transaction number
## ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
